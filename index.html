Booking engine tool

The booking engine is a free of charge technological tool created by Wanup for our partner hotels. When you implement it on your website, you can increase your direct bookings and lower your commissions. You can read all the details about the booking engine here.

What does the tool offer?

It provides you with a booking solution that you can easily integrate on your site. Your guests who book via this tool become Wanup members and remain at 0% commission whenever they re-book in your hotel.

It's also a way to reduce your overall commissions. Once you sign up enough new members to reach your monthly acquisition target (MAT*), the commission for your other bookings with Wanup will be reduced by 2% for the rest of the month.

Depending on the layout of your website, you can choose between 5 modern and functional types.

* To calculate your property’s MAT, multiply the number of rooms in your hotel by 2.5.

 

Link

Easy to integrate within a block of text or in any other place you need a link.

The link brings the guest to your page on wanup.com, and yours alone – users won’t be able to search other hotels through this page.

 

Get the code:

 


  1. Login with your username and password at: https://ext.wanup.com
  2. Click on the Marketing section in the menu to the left of the screen
  3. Click ‘Widgets’ in the top menu of the marketing section
  4. Select ‘Widget type: link’
  5. Choose the preferred language from the drop-down box
  6. Type your desired text
  7. Choose the colour you prefer for the link
  8. Click ‘Get widget’ to generate the code
  9. Copy and paste to your website

Frequently asked questions


1. Where does the booking engine send guests when they click on it?

- The booking engine generates a site for the hotel, where users are able to book your available rooms, with an option to redeem their travel cash. From this page, guests cannot search for other hotels in the club – they’ll only be able to book yours.

2. Are other Wanup Hotels shown in my booking engine?

- No, other properties are not shown through the search engine. Only your accommodation profile is shown and it’s the only property that can be booked through it.

3. Can new members join Wanup through the booking engine?

- Yes, guests can sign up through the booking engine at the time of booking. An advantage is that these new members will always be at a 0% commission for your hotel and will contribute towards your monthly acquisition goal to lower your overall commissions with the club.

4. What is the cost of getting the booking engine?

- None. The tool is completely free, and you will also have free technical support during implementation, if you need it.

5. ¿Puede estar el motor de reserva en los distintos idiomas de mi página web?

- Sí, podemos crear un motor de reserva por cada uno de los 7 idiomas disponibles (inglés, castellano, italiano, francés, alemán, portugués, holandés).

6. Will the booking engine influence the speed of my webpage?

- No. The tool is light and won’t affect the speed of your webpage in any way.

7. How can I centre the booking engine on my webpage?

- You can add the following properties to the style tag of the code: display: block; margin: auto; But remember, if you need, we provide free technical support for the implementation of the booking engine. Just contact us at……

8. Is it possible to change the size of the booking engine?

- The height and width of the widget are defined by default in the style tag. These can be changed directly in the code and adapted to the web. It is important to respect the minimums established for each template.

9. Can we change the colour of the booking engine?

- Yes. There are 5 colours available (red, yellow, blue, green, grey). The details are shown above with examples.

10. Can I generate a native button that redirects to the booking engine?

- Yes, you can use the booking engine link or button options in order to generate links and native buttons.
